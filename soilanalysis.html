Soil Analysis for Farmers

1. Clay Soil

Clay soil is dense and retains moisture, making it fertile but heavy. It is ideal for crops like rice and wheat.

2. Sandy Soil

Sandy soil drains quickly and is warm. It is excellent for root crops like carrots and potatoes but requires more water.

3. Loamy Soil

Loamy soil is a balanced mixture of sand, silt, and clay, offering good drainage and fertility. It is suitable for most crops.

4. Silt Soil

Silt soil is smooth and retains moisture well. It is ideal for growing vegetables and crops that require consistent moisture.

5. Peaty Soil

Peaty soil is rich in organic matter and moisture. It is best for growing specific crops like blueberries and cranberries.
