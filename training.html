Training Programs for Farmers

1) Custom Harvesting

The custom harvesting program is an opportunity to see the beautiful country of the land, while operating new combine technology. Get exposed to a large custom harvesting operation and learn the most efficient harvesting methods alongside general maintenance skills and commodity marketing.

This agriculture training program is available in: Guntur, Andhra pradesh

2) Field Crops

The field crops training program will allow a participant to gain hands-on experience during the planting, growing, and harvest seasons and to learn the latest in fertilizer, pesticide, and herbicide applications. Field crop training programs will include the opportunity to operate farm machinery and have exposure to the latest farming technologies.

This agriculture training program is available in: vizag, Andhra pradesh
Request for Training

3) Vegetable Production

Vegetable Production and Marketing interns will gain hands-on experience with seed production, planting, disease and weed control, irrigation and soil management, fertilizer usage, packaging, and marketing. This training will help operations grow a wide variety of vegetables, such as sweet corn, potatoes, tomatoes, peppers, squash, carrots, broccoli, beets, etc.

This agriculture training program is available in: vijayawada, Andhra pradesh
Request for Training

4) Fruit Production

Orchard training program placements involve small fruit, soft fruit, and apples. Whether you are looking for small farming or a large commercial operation, this program will provide hands-on experience and knowledge of the orchard industry in the United States, all while gaining skills in production.

This agriculture training program is available in: Bapatla, Andhra pradesh
